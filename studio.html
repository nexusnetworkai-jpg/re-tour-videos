The fine print

Your photos, your rights. By ordering you confirm you own or are licensed to use the photos you send. We use them solely to produce your tour and delete them within 30 days of delivery. We never resell or repost your images.

Music. Every tour uses audio we generate ourselves from scratch (synthesized tones — no sampled or third-party tracks), so there is no external music licence for you to buy or clear. Prefer your own track? Send it and we'll use it instead.

Refunds. One free revision is included. If you're still not satisfied, email us within 7 days of delivery for a full refund via Stripe.

Privacy. Payment is handled by Stripe; we never see your card details. We store only your email and photos, for as long as needed to deliver and support your order.

Common questions

What photo formats and sizes do you accept?

JPG, PNG, or HEIC — anything exported from your MLS, phone, or camera. Minimum 1 MP per photo (any modern phone is fine). Send 6–15 photos for best results via a Google Drive or Dropbox link, or just attach them to your email.

Can I upload the finished video to Zillow, MLS, or YouTube?

Yes to all three. We deliver a standard MP4 that uploads directly to Zillow, Realtor.com, and most MLS video fields. You also get a vertical 9:16 cut optimised for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts at no extra charge.

What exactly appears on my video?

Smooth Ken Burns pan/zoom on every photo, room labels (Exterior, Living Room, Kitchen, etc.), a title card with the property address and key stats, royalty-free background music, and a closing card with your name/logo if you want it. Tell us your preferences in the requirements form after checkout.

How do I send you my photos after I pay?

After Stripe confirms your payment you'll land on a receipt page that shows the delivery email address. Just reply to that page's confirmation email — or email us directly at nexus.network.ai+tours@gmail.com — with a Google Drive / Dropbox link or the photos attached. We'll confirm receipt and deliver your tour within 24–48 hours.

Does the 3-pack expire?

No expiry — use all three tours whenever you need them, on any listings. Each additional tour just takes another photo email.
