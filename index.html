Common questions

What if my photos are shot on a phone?

Phone photos are fine — most of our orders come from iPhone/Android shots. Landscape orientation works best; portrait (vertical) shots are cropped. Resolution doesn't need to be high: anything above 1 MP renders cleanly.

How do I tell you which room is which?

In your email with the photos, number the files (01_kitchen.jpg, 02_living.jpg) or write us a list. We label them exactly as you name them. Unlabelled photos get generic labels (Room 1, Room 2) — we always include a free revision if something's off.

Can I use this on MLS, Zillow, or Realtor.com?

Yes. The video is an MP4 file you own completely. You can upload it anywhere — MLS listing portals, YouTube, your website, or embed it in emails. The vertical 9:16 version is sized for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

What does "AI Studio" mean? Is there a human involved?

The video is produced entirely by software — we openly disclose this on every tour (you'll see a small "AI Studio" credit). No human editor, no videographer. That's how we deliver in 24 hours for $89. We never pretend a human produced it.

Can I use my own branding / logo?

Yes — tell us your agent name, brokerage name, and any logo file (PNG with transparent background) when you send your photos. We overlay it on the outro card. White-label clients get an unbranded video with no Orion credit.

What exactly is the 24-hour guarantee?

The clock starts the moment we receive your photos. If your finished video isn't delivered to your inbox within 24 hours of that email, we refund you immediately and you keep the video when it arrives anyway. In practice, most orders complete in under 2 hours.

What if I don't like the result?

One free revision is included — just reply with what you'd like changed (room label, photo order, etc.) and we re-render within 24h. If you're still not happy after that, reply within 7 days for a full Stripe refund. No form, no explanation required.

Do you shoot drone / aerial footage?

No — we work from photos you already have. If you send an aerial shot from your drone or a previous shoot, we include it as a photo in the tour. We don't dispatch a drone.
