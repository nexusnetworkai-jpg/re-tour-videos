The Free DIY Path: AI Narration with ElevenLabs + ffmpeg

If you're comfortable with a terminal and want professional AI narration at zero cost, this workflow produces results that rival $75 Fiverr videos. The learning curve is real, but once set up it takes about 10 minutes per listing.

  1. Write your script — Use a template (see our Template Pack below) or paste your listing highlights into ChatGPT and ask for a 90-second narration script.
  2. Generate voiceover — Paste the script into ElevenLabs (free tier = ~12 videos/month). Choose "Sarah" (warm, professional) or "George" (luxury, authoritative). Download the MP3.
  3. Prep photos — Rename MLS exports to photo_01.jpg, photo_02.jpg, etc. Order them: best exterior first, best room last.
  4. Run ffmpeg — One command assembles the slideshow + voiceover into an HD MP4. Full recipe in our Template Pack.
  5. Upload — Directly to Zillow/MLS, or to YouTube (creates a search-indexed listing page for the address).

Frequently Asked Questions

Do real estate listings with video actually perform better?

Yes — NAR data consistently shows listings with video receive significantly more inquiries than those without. Video helps buyers self-qualify before scheduling a showing, which also means better-quality showings for agents.

Can I upload AI-generated tour videos to MLS/Zillow?

Yes. Most MLS systems accept MP4 video uploads alongside photos. Zillow, Realtor.com, and Redfin all support listing videos. There are no restrictions on AI-generated content for property marketing.

What's the minimum number of photos I need?

5 photos is enough for a 30-second video. For a standard 60-second tour, 8–10 photos work well. Premium 90-second videos benefit from 15–20 images.

Is Animoto worth $16/month if I only list 2–3 properties a month?

At 2–3 listings/month, that's $5–8 per video — a good deal. If you list fewer than one property a month, consider per-video AI services instead so you're not paying a subscription for idle months.

How does the DIY ElevenLabs + ffmpeg workflow compare to Animoto in output quality?

With AI narration, the DIY output is actually superior for engagement — narrated tours outperform music-only slideshows. Animoto wins on ease of use; the DIY route wins on quality and cost if you invest the 2-hour setup time.

Do I need to disclose that the voiceover is AI-generated?

There are currently no legal requirements in the US or EU to disclose AI voiceover in property marketing videos. However, the script itself must be accurate — don't describe features the property doesn't have.
